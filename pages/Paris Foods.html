Culinary Journey Through Paris: Must-Try Foods and Where to Find Them

By Sebastian Santillan | Published on June 15, 2024

Paris, revered as the epicenter of gastronomy, beckons food enthusiasts with its unparalleled culinary offerings. From iconic pastries and artisanal cheeses to hearty bistro classics, Parisian cuisine is a feast for the senses. Join us on a gastronomic adventure as we explore the must-try foods in Paris, each dish embodying French culinary artistry at its finest.

Croissants:

  • Description: Light, flaky pastries known for their buttery goodness, a quintessential Parisian breakfast staple.
  • Where to Try: Visit renowned bakeries like Maison Pichard and Du Pain et des Idées for an authentic croissant experience.
  • Experience: Delight in the delicate layers and melt-in-your-mouth texture of a freshly baked croissant, paired perfectly with a café au lait.

Baguette:

  • Description: The iconic French bread with a crisp crust and soft, airy interior, beloved across Paris.
  • Where to Try: Award-winning boulangeries such as Le Grenier à Pain and Poilâne, where artisanal bread-making thrives.
  • Experience: Enjoy the aroma of freshly baked bread, savoring the contrast of crunchy crust and tender crumb that defines the perfect baguette.

Escargots de Bourgogne:

  • Description: Burgundy snails cooked with garlic, parsley, and butter, offering a taste of classic French cuisine.
  • Where to Try: Traditional bistros like L'Escargot Montorgueil and Chez Georges, known for their expertise in preparing escargots.
  • Experience: Indulge in the tender texture and decadent flavors of escargots, paired with crusty French bread to soak up the savory sauce.

Steak Frites:

  • Description: A beloved French dish featuring grilled steak served with golden, crispy French fries.
  • Where to Try: Iconic bistros such as Le Relais de l’Entrecôte and Café de Paris, famed for their steak frites offerings.
  • Experience: Delight in the succulent steak, cooked to perfection and accompanied by the irresistible crunch of hand-cut fries, often accompanied by a secret sauce.

Cheese:

  • Description: France boasts a rich tapestry of cheeses, from creamy Brie and pungent Camembert to tangy Roquefort.
  • Where to Try: Explore specialty cheese shops like Fromagerie Laurent Dubois and Androuet, where cheese is celebrated as an art form.
  • Experience: Sample a diverse selection of cheeses, each with its own unique profile and history, complemented by freshly baked bread and fine wine.

Macarons:

  • Description: Elegant almond meringue cookies filled with luscious ganache, buttercream, or fruit preserves.
  • Where to Try: Patisseries such as Ladurée and Pierre Hermé, renowned for their exquisite macaron creations.
  • Experience: Indulge in the vibrant colors and delicate flavors of macarons, ranging from classic flavors like vanilla and chocolate to innovative combinations that tantalize the palate.

Coq au Vin:

  • Description: A hearty French stew featuring chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms, onions, and bacon.
  • Where to Try: Rustic bistros like Le Bistrot Paul Bert and Chez René, where traditional French comfort food shines.
  • Experience: Enjoy the tender chicken infused with the robust flavors of red wine and aromatic herbs, served alongside crusty baguette to mop up every savory drop.

Crêpes:

  • Description: Thin, delicate pancakes that can be filled with sweet or savory ingredients, a versatile Parisian delight.
  • Where to Try: Crêperies like Breizh Café and La Crêperie de Josselin, offering both traditional and inventive crêpe creations.
  • Experience: Indulge in sweet crêpes filled with Nutella, fresh fruit, or caramel, or savor savory galettes filled with ham, cheese, and eggs, showcasing the versatility of this beloved French treat.

Conclusion

Paris, with its culinary treasures steeped in history and passion, invites you to embark on a journey of taste and tradition. Whether you're savoring the buttery layers of a croissant or the robust flavors of coq au vin, each bite tells a story of French culinary excellence. Dive into these must-try foods to discover why Paris remains a global beacon of gastronomy. Bon appétit!
